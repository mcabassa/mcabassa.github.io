Representing average rating by genre

Visual narrative mockups

One of the first ideas that came to me when I was exploring possible visualizations with the IMDb dataset was a graph made of concentric circles. I could use different rings for different titles, the entire circle to represent the rating, and the layer to illustrate the year of release.

I also thought about sorting the series by genre and representing the different genres using small multiples so that I could uncover some hidden patterns between genres.

Finally, I repeated this idea twice, changing the thickness of the circle instead of its entirety, and also adding small circles for each circle, like planets, so that I could also change its diameter and color hue.
