Top rated series

Project Overview

The idea behind this visualization was exploring and uncovering trends by genre, between the top rated series according to IMDb.

Details

The visualization consists in a series of small multiples, one for each genre, which should uncover any rating trends between them.

Outcome

This charts could have the potential of both being attractive a and useful. I will continue to explore this path probably using D3.js.
